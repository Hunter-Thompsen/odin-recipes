Southern Fried Chicken

Description:

Crispy and flavorful, Southern Fried Chicken is a beloved classic that boasts a perfectly seasoned, golden-brown crust enveloping tender, juicy chicken. It's the ultimate comfort food that brings the taste of the South to your plate with each succulent bite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken pieces (such as thighs and drumsticks)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the buttermilk and hot sauce. Add the chicken pieces, ensuring they are fully coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for the best results.
  2. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil in a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven to 350째F (175째C).
  4. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk mixture and allow any excess to drip off.
  5. Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, pressing the flour onto the chicken to ensure a good coating.
  6. Carefully place the chicken in the hot oil, skin-side down. Fry for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is golden brown and the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
  7. Remove the fried chicken and place it on a wire rack to drain excess oil. Allow it to cool slightly before serving.
